LEGOLAND

Legoland is a theme park ever opened in Asia since its inception. The official opening of the theme park was launched by Sultan Ibrahim Ismail on 22 September 2012.It will be the core of an integrated complex measuring 5,500,000 square feet (510,000 m2). This area is also a Nusa Cemerlang industrial park located in Iskandar Malaysiai.e. an economic area consisting of lifestyle centers such as retail, offices, hotels, apartment services and residential units.his theme park, Lego -themed attractions are built near the area. Next, the Lego water theme park is a project that opened in mid -2013 as well as the Legoland Hotel. The hotel was also the first in Asia when its opening began in the first half of 2014. The hotel was built under a management agreement between the companies. LL Themed Hotel Sdn. Bhd. is a joint venture company owned by Destination Resorts and Hotels Sdn. PULAU RAWA

Pulau Rawa has clean white sandy beaches. Clear water surrounds all the beaches on the islands. This is partly because it faces the open sea, making its beach hit by waves that pull mud from its beach sand. It is owned by the Sultan of Johor's family, has two resorts that can provide accommodation facilities to visitors, namely Rawa Island Resort and the smaller resort Alang's Rawa. Visitors who want to go to Rawa Island must take the speedboat service from Mersing jetty before reaching the island.

Rawa Island is accessible by a 20 minute speedboat ride from Mersing, Johor. While the majority of our guests choose to drive from Kuala Lumpur or Singapore, we are also able to arrange for transport with third party operators to and from Mersing. Secured car parking facilities are available at our Mersing Front Office.

JOHOR PREMIUM OUTLETS

Johor Premium Outlets (or JPO ) is a major shopping mall in Iskandar Malaysia, Johor , Malaysia. The mall is located in Kulai District . Johor Premium Outlets is the first premium outlets built in Southeast Asia. Johor Premium Outlets officially opened on 11 December 2011. There have 150 outlet stores that houses branded and international standard items such as Coach, Adidas, Nike, Armani, Salvator and others and can get a discount of 25% to 70%. The mall can also be seen via the North-South Highway after the North Senai Junction. Travel time from the Johor Causeway is the next 45 minutes via the Johor Bahru East Dispersion Scheme Highway and the North-South Highway . For anyone coming from Kuala Lumpur will take 3 to 4 hours via the North-South Highway. Johor Premium Outlets shopping mall is also connected to Senai International Airport via the Second Link Highway which takes 5 to 10 minutes. It open daily from 10am to 10pm.
