PULAU PINANG

BUKIT BENDERA

Bukit Bendera is a parliamentary area in the state of Pulau Pinang , Malaysia . Pulau Pinang is located between the lands of Kedah and Perak in the north of Peninsular Malaysia. At 830 m above Georgetown , the peak of Bukit Bendera provides a comfortable base that allows travelers to escape the biting heat below. In general, this hill is 5 degrees Celsius cooler than the sea line. From the top, travelers can see amazing views down the entire island and even to the main land across the sea. There is a pleasant garden, old-style cottages, restaurants and inns, a Hindu temple , and a Muslim Mosque on top. Pulau Pinang Hill is actually dubbed Bulit Strawberi because it is also a producer of strawberries . The shape of the earth's surface and cold temperatures have made strawberries grow well. The stub that reaches the top is easy via the Waterfall Botanical Gardens on foot. The official name of this hill is Bukit Pulau Pinang, and it is still generally called Bukit Pulau Pinang

ESCAPE THEMEPARK

ESCAPE Penang hosts 40 exciting rides & attractions - rope & obstacle courses, ziplines, climbing, diving towers and the Guinness World Records Longest Tube Water Slide - immersed in the rich rainforest of Teluk Bahang, Penang Island.

ESCAPE is the fun destination with exciting rides and attractions. Hosted with nature in mind. Located in Teluk Bahang, Penang, Malaysia, ESCAPE re-introduces outdoor play in a natural environment so to give the visitor an appreciation of the world around them and shows that there's no age limit to having fun.

Opening Hours: Tue - Sun, 10am - 6pm

THE TOP KOMTAR SKYWALK

The KOMTAR Tower in George Town is Penang's tallest skyscraper, standing at almost 249 metres tall. KOMTAR, an acronym for Kompleks Tun Abdul Razak, is actually a multi-purpose complex containing administrative offices of the Penang state government (including the Chief Minister of Penang's office) and the Penang Island City Council, retail outlets and a transportation hub. Due to its towering height, it serves as one of the most prominent landmarks on Penang Island. KOMTAR tower was completed in 1988; at the time, it was Asia's third tallest skyscraper.The U-shaped 16 metre-long glass walkway offers visitors an unimpeded panoramic view of the cityscape of George Town, its surrounding suburbs such as Tanjung Tokong, Pulau Tikus and Air Itam, and the Penang Hill.The Komtar Skywalk can be accessed between 11am and 10pm daily.
