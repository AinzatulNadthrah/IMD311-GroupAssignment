KELLIE'S CASTLE

Kellie’s Castle House or Kellas House was built in 1905 by a prominent Scottish rubber farmer named William Kellie Smith . The three -storey building was built according to the design of a Moghul palace in the city of Agra , India . The builders of the building were specially brought from Madras.The building has 14 rooms, a basement and the secret tunnel is also equipped with the first elevator built in Malaya . On the second floor will be built a tennis court with a cement floor.A bridge across the Kinta River was built for visitors to easily get to Kellie’s Palace. A eatery and souvenir shop and car park were built by the West Kinta District Council in 1990.Kellie's Castle is often the location of the filming of dramas and movies . Among them is the film Anna And The King starring Chow Yuen Fatt. Kellie castle opening times - 9 am - 6 pm

PULAU PANGKOR

Pangkor Island is a tropical island and has several interesting tourist attractions. Among the places that are the focus of tourists. Pangkor Island can be explored on foot or by land and sea vehicles. The surroundings are ideal for sea activities such as swimming, snorkelling , kayaking, and fishing. Pangkor Laut Resort owned by YTL Hotels on the west side of Pasir Bogak Beach is a luxury private resort of international standard. There are few things visiotors can enjoyed during the vacation which are:

  • Spend the afternoon on an empty beach
  • Colorful communities in Chinatown
  • Explore the island from a kayak
  • Discover a thriving marine world on a snorkeling trip
  • Ride a boat to Pangkor’s most elegant restaurant

TASIK CERMIN

Its beauty seems to be hidden, until many of us do not know the existence of a lake named 'Tasik Cermin' near Simpang Pulai here. However, Tasik Cermin is a beautiful lake hidden and has its own uniqueness and no doubt it is one of the new places among lovers. nature. The water of this mirror lake is green and shiny as if it looks like a mirror and sparkles. There are times when there is a reflection of the surrounding mountain shadows on the surface of the lake.
