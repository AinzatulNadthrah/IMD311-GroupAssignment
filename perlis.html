PERLIS

GUA KELAM

Gua Kelam used to be famous for ore mines that produce high quality ore. This cave is also a connecting road between the people of Wang Kelian and Kaki Bukit. Gua Kelam is named in conjunction with its location at the foot of the hill and the dark atmosphere that always surrounds this cave. An underground river about 400 meters long flowing through a range of limestone can be seen while passing through this cave. This underground river is a phenomenon of natural occurrence. Electricity is provided for visitors to enjoy the beauty in the cave. In 1935, an Englishman realized that the river route here was a good way to transport tin ore from a mine near this river through an underground cave to Kaki Bukit.

TAMAN REKREASI BUKIT AYER

Bukit Ayer Amusement Forest is located 12 km from Kangar Town . Several facilities are available for such as information center, chalets, campsite, children's playground and parking facilities. To reach this amenity forest, visitors can use their own vehicles or public transport such as buses or taxis from Kangar City. Waterfalls and rivers that flow clear naturally beautify the atmosphere. Downstream there is a beautiful swimming pool for visitors. There is also a swimming pool for children. The campsite which accommodates about 100 participants is equipped with a cooking area and a clean water source. For those who love extreme activities there are trails that can be used for that purpose. Here there are also children's playgrounds, food stalls, bathrooms, prayer rooms and rest huts.
